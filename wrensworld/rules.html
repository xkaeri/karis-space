Rules

We strive for clear and consistent moderation, but also allow for flexibility to interpret rules based on their intended spirit, not just their exact wording. If we believe something abides by the spirit of a rule, we may allow it even if it would technically break a rule as written and vice versa.

Moderators have the discretion to issue warnings, mutes, and bans based on the severity and frequency of rule violations. Deliberately trying to manipulate the system by breaking rules will result in consequences.

This server has an automod that automatically deletes swears. Getting an direct message by YAGPDB does not mean an immediate strike, its simply an infortmation on what is allowed to be said and what is not!

Abide by Discord's Terms of Service and Guidelines.

  1. Be respectful
    Don't be disrespectful, hostile or sexualize other users, mods or Wren.
    Don't be parasocial toward other users, mods and don't be overly parasocial towards Wren
    Don't ship real people. Thats weird and icky.
  2. No discrimination
    Don't imply, bait, reference, or otherwise indirectly hint at offensive matters.
    Don't be racist, homophobic, transphobic, intentionally misgendering, bigoted, etc.
    This is a safe community. Everyone is welcome here.
  3. No controversial topics
    Don't talk about any drama or controversial topics in the Discord, like politics and religion.
    Don't discuss in detail the usage or ownership of guns, weapons, drugs, or drug paraphernalia.
    Don't include any controversial imagery in profile pictures or banners.
  4. No adult/NSFW content
    No adult, NSFW, or suggestive content. This ia a 13+ server, not a Dating/NSFW server - keep it clean.
    No suggestive profile pictures or banners.
  5. Chatting
    Use the appropriate channels for their purpose.
    Don't spam or flood channels with messages, images, or emojis.
    Don't unnecessary ping the mods or Wren.
  6. Modding
    Don't argue with the mods or Wren's decisions. Open a ticket if you would like to talk about it.
    Don't try to manipulate the system by breaking rules on purpose with the intention of getting punished.
    Don't share mod-only information or actions with non-mods, doing so will result in a punishment.
  7. No advertising or self-promotion
    Don't advertise other Discord servers, social media, or content.
  8. Follow the Hoyo Terms of Service
    Don't share or discuss anything that might violate the Hoyo Terms of Service.
    Do not engage in discussions or activities that may hurt Wren's Hoyocreator status.
